Happy National Flowers Day

From the moment I met you, I was captivated,
Addicted to your laughter, attracted in ways I can't explain.
I care about you more than words can convey,
Think of you constantly, appreciate you more each day.

You're the melody in my every thought,
The poetry that dances in my heart's core,
Your voice, a symphony of love and grace,
Your presence, a masterpiece in every space.

No matter the distance that separates us,
My thoughts always find their way back to you,
In every sight, sound, scent, taste, and touch,
Reminders of you, my love, ever so much.

Happy National Flowers Day, Mycha,
May this day be as beautiful as you are,
A celebration of love, in every bloom and hue,
Forever grateful for the gift of loving you.
